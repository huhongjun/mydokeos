There are many courses already freely available for use with Open Elms.

We invite you to make your courses similarly available for use on the Open Elms site.

One stipulation to distributing your courses to other organisations is that the content must only be downloaded via the Open Elms website.

This enables us to ensure the quality standards are maintained.

Please visit the site at www.openelms.org to download courses.

Courses currently available include employee induction, Health and Safety and compliance.
