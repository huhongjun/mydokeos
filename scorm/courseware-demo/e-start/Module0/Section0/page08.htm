Administration
The purpose of our administration department is to assist our customers with timely feedback when requested through the mail, e-mail or by fax.

In addition the administrative department will book meetings, take notes and generally assist with the day to day running of other departments within the company.
Sales & Marketing
Our sales and marketing department is made up of account servicing and marketing professionals with extensive experience in the local and overseas markets. The most outstanding trait is their creativity and flexibility in working with clients to meet their needs.
Research and Development
Our Research and Development Department is often searching for new needs in the industry. We regularly work in joint venture development projects with our customers and pride ourselves on designing simple machinery and systems to perform difficult tasks.
Production.
Our production team works around the clock in two shifts to deliver prompt service and premium quality. Team spirit is stressed and a great sense of efficiency and effectiveness is instilled in each one of the workers. High productivity is evident.
HR
Our aim is to promote a culture which supports learning and continuous development by helping our staff improve their skills and knowledge – as well as maximise their potential.
Finance
Our Finance Department is responsible for all financial and contractual aspects of the company, including producing monthly management and annual accounts, as well as operating and checking financial controls. It also manages all purchase and sales invoice, and processing of salaries.
Other
There are many other activities carried out by the company including Facilities Management, health and Safety, IT Support, Corporate Services, Catering etc. Contact your line manager for further information.
