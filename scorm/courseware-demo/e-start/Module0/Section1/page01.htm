Employment Contract
Your employment with the company is subject to your employment contract. You should have already received your contract containing the terms and conditions of your employment. The contract of employment has been sent to you in duplicate, one copy is for yourself, the other to be returned to the Human Resources Department.

Probation Period
The probationary period enables both you and ExampleCompany to ascertain your suitability for the job. During this period, either party with one week's notice can terminate employment. At the end of your probationary period, a formal appraisal will take place with your direct line manager. This appraisal allows both you and your manager to provide each other with feedback on how you feel you are progressing.
