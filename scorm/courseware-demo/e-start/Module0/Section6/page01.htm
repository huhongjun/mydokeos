The Purchasing Department is responsible for company procurement..

The following procedure must be followed when you wish to purchase an item:
Step 1
Complete a purchase order request form detailing the item you wish to purchase

This is only applicable for items with a value over £50 exc. VAT, if the item costs more than £1,000 additional quotations must be obtained.
Step 2
This document will need to be signed off by your line manager.
Step 3
Send the purchase order request form to the Finance department where it will be given a reference number and be logged.
Step 4
Confirmation that the purchase order has been approved and processed will be sent to you.
Step 5
When the delivery arrives, the purchase order form is checked against the delivery note to ensure that the ordered goods are correct:
Step 6
The invoice and delivery note are returned to the purchasing department for filing.
