The growth of internet services such as chat rooms and dating websites has led to a new type of interpersonal communication.

When you are in a chat room or newsgroup, anyone has the potential to read what you’re saying. Don’t give anything away, especially your personal details, including your real name. Even the slightest detail might lead to someone being able to find out personal things about you. Never arrange to meet anyone through the internet. If you do, always ensure it is in a public place and there are plenty of people around. Tell a friend you are going and arrange a friend to phone you.

Ignore anything that makes you uncomfortable in a chat room, news group or e-mail message. If the content is really offensive consider contacting the Internet company or even the police.
Whenever you are going out, try to tell someone where you are going and when you expect to be back.

If you are travelling to another destination try to let that person know you have arrived safely.


If anything is holding you up, this will alert others to any potential problems.
