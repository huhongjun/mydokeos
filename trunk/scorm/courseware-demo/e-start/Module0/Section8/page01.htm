e-start deals not only with e-learning. The system also give you the option of adding checklists to the end of the induction as well.

Questions such as "Have you received the company brochure?", "Do you know where your nearest muster point is?" or "Have you received a welcome interview?" can all be asked. Depending upon the user's answer, the relevant management personnel are informed of any tasks that need to be fulfilled.

Do not worry if you feel this functionality is beyond your current requriements. This can be left out of your induction and added at a later stage if you wish.
