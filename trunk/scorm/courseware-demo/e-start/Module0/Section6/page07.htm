ExampleCompany's internal communication objective is to develop and maintain an effective flow of information across the Company. This is to ensure all employees have a clear understanding of the company's aims, current business conditions and are able to feel part of the Organisation.

The increased use and availability of the Internet, Intranet, Internal and external e-mail present enormous business opportunities through increased efficiency and communications. However it must also be recognised that there is a degree of potential risk involved in its use.

Certain breaches of confidentiality could result in legal proceedings being brought by third parties against not only the individual, but also the Company. Inappropriate use of technology will be investigated. Should misuse be found, disciplinary action, which may range from Internet access being revoked to dismissal, will take place.
