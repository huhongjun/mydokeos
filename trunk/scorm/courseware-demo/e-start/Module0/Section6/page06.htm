If you have a grievance, you should, first attempt to resolve the problem informally. It may be a sufficient to explain to the person how you are feeling to stop the unwanted behaviour.

In cases where it would be too awkward or difficult to do this then seek help from your line manager or the HR department.
If their behaviour persists you should report it to the HR department. We advise that you detail the incident(s) in writing, with details of any dates and times included. The HR department and management will then commence an investigation and proceed accordingly.
The details of the case will be analysed and further investigations may need to be carried out. The case will be dealt with confidentiality, only those directly involved within the investigation will have access to the information.
If it is decided that a misdemeanour has taken place then appropriate punitive measures will be taken in line with the company’s disciplinary procedures.
