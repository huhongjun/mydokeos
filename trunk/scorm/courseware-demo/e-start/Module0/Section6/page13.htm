The induction is constructed from provided course content (PowerPoint & Word Documents and media such as photos and video.)

You can expand the induction to include content from any of our highly interactive courses.

Press the [More] button to see the courses currently available (contact e-learning WMB for the latest list and details of more specialised courses.)

Data Protection: This course will teach you the basics of the Data Protection Act, how it works and how it applies to the work you do.

Press the Data Protection link to see the course content.

Stress : This course helps identify stress in the workplace and to tackle it at its source. The e-learning also offers guidance on techniques to reduce stress.

Equality & Diversity : This course teaches you what to do if you feel you are being discriminated or treated unfairly in anyway, how to avoid discrimination in the workplace and what the consequences are if you are found guilty of discrimination.

Security : This course teaches the user how to comply with a company's security procedures. As with all the featured e-learning courses, aptitude and risk assessment are included in the elearning solution.

Contractor Induction : When inviting contractors on site to carry out work it is important that they are inducted into the safety and security procedures for the company. This course includes vital security and safety information which is necessary to understand prior to starting work.

Personal Safety : This module shows you how to ensure your own personal safety in and outside of your workspace.

Office Safety : This course shows you how to carry out your own ergonomics workstation assessment. The course includes an aptitude test and a complete ergonomic assessment.

Manual Handling : This course is an introduction to manual handling and lifting techniques.

Management of Health & Safety : This course instructs line managers in effectively carrying out their health and safety duties under the Management of Health and Safety at Work legislation.

Fire Safety : Evacuation procedures and basic fire safety are covered in this module.

Driver Safety : This e-learning course outlines the key issues behind staying safe behind the wheel of your car. The e-learning instructs you on the most common causes of road accidents and instructs you on how to avoid them.
