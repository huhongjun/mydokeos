Petty cash should only be used to purchase goods with a value of less than £50.00. Purchases in excess of £50.00 should go through the proper expense process.

Petty cash should also not be used as an advance on expenses to any member of staff.

In some cases where there is an urgent need for money, petty cash can be used to purchase larger items or advance employees. Consult the petty cash officer in such circumstances.

Petty cash boxes are kept locked. Petty cash officers are the only people authorised to access the boxes.

The person, who is claiming petty cash must provide a receipt for goods ensuring the receipt shows the vendors' VAT number, this should be attached to the petty cash form.
