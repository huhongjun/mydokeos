ExampleCompany actively encourages staff to take their annual holiday entitlement and public holidays. All full-time employees working a standard week of 37½ hours are allocated 22 days annual leave per annum on joining the company.
This will increase on a yearly basis by 1 day per year of service until you reach 25 days per annum. The Holiday year runs from 1st January to 31st December.

    • Public Holidays
    Full-time employees working in the United Kingdom are entitled to take the recognised public holidays in the United Kingdom.
    If you happen to be working overseas, you should adopt the public holidays of your host country.
    How do I request Annual Leave?
    All Annual Leave requests are subject to approval from your direct line manager and in some cases the request may not be approved. Annual Leave requests must be made in writing using an Annual Leave Request Form (link to annual request form). To ensure smooth running of the team and business, requests must be made at least one week in advance prior to the commencement date of your leave.
    Cancelling Annual Leave
    If you need to cancel a period of annual leave you will need to complete a Cancellation of Annual Leave Form, which will need to be authorised by your line manager.
    If you do not take annual leave
    A total of 5 days Annual leave may be carried over from one holiday year to the next, providing that these days are taken within the first 3 months of the new holiday year. If you wish to carry over any annual leave this must be authorised by your manager and human resources.
    What if I want to take save up my holiday and take it in a large block?
    Employees are requested to take a minimum of 2 blocks of 5 working days annual leave during the holiday year, These blocks can be taken either separately or together. Holiday requests for periods longer than a 10 working day block, are authorised at the discretion of your direct line manager & human resources.
