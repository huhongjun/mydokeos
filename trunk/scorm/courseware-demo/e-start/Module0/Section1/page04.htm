Flat-mate: "I know them, it’s supposed to be an incredibly laid back and fun place to work. Apparently they have meetings on beanbags.

You want to go there in cargo pants and trainers"
What should Sally do?
Dress down to make the client feel at home

or
Wear normal office attire
