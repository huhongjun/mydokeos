CEO Andrew Onymous
Andrew Onymous is involved in the management of day-to-day operations, including liaison between the Company and its customers. He ensures that employees comply with the industrys rules and regulations.
Mr. Onymous has been President, Chief Executive Officer and a Director of the Company since 1981. He holds a Master of Business Administration degree. Mr. Onymous, an accountant by training, has comprehensive knowledge of import-export markets and of financial operations which has enabled him to work in fast-paced, highly diversified environments, orchestrating international transactions involving millions of dollars with most of the major multi-national conglomerates.
Vice President Dr. Gerald Wiseman
Dr. Gerald Wiseman is involved in oversight of research and development, product integrity and manufacture of all the companys products..
Dr. Wiseman brings over twenty-five years of industry expertise to the Company. From 1989 to the present, Dr Wiseman has been involved with government steering committees to ensure good practice within the industry..
Kevin Rhee - Vice President & Director International Sales
Kevin Rhee is involved in the oversight of international marketing, sales network and distribution, supervision of global sales offices, administration and personnel.
Mr. Rhee has been the Company's overseas marketing director since January, 1992. He will assume responsibility for worldwide marketing of products distributed and/or manufactured by the Company. Mr. Rhee was previously a self-employed sales representative and operated in the United States. Mr. Rhee was also formerly employed as General Manager for the New Industries Office of Saudi Arabia.
Gilbert Smith - Development Director.
Joined company 1970, held a number of positions including director of European Operations. In 1993 he became Quality, Research and Development director and member of the Executive Committee, where he is also responsible for coordinating quality and development issues with European subsidiaries and partners Spain and Italy
Henry Brown - Purchasing Director
Started his career in purchasing with Ford, then joined the company. Held several positions in purchasing within the organisatoin, before his appointment to current position and executive committee membership in 1995.
Luc Smith - marketing Director
Began career with the company. Has held a series of positions in sales in several of the Group companies, the last of which was Marketing Director of fresh dairy products with ExampleCompany in Argentina from 1997-99. Was appointed a member of executive committee in January 2000.
Jane Haslett - Financial Director
Joined the company in 1985 and held several positions in the finance and management accounting departments before her appointment as Financial Director and membership of the executive Committee in 1998. Oversees the Direction Administrative et Financire which includes the following departments: accounting, management accounting, legal affairs, information technology and the treasury function.
Lucy Wetherspoon - Human Resources Director
Appointed to current position on the executive committee in 1997. After working as a recruitment consultant, joined the Company in 1983, where she held various positions in human resources and logistics in five different areas, the last of which was a Human Resources Director in SA.
Patrick Tabary - Industrial Director
Became member of the Executive Committee in September 2001. Joined the company in the human resources department in 1984 then held several positions in the industrial department including Logistics Director. Has held his current position of Industrial Director for six years.
