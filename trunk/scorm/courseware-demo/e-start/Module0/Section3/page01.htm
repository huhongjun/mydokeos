Medical Insurance
A voluntary Medical Insurance Plan enables you to insure against the cost of private medical treatment and hospitalization.

Cover may be extended to partners and dependants, however this would be at additional cost to yourself.
Travel Insurance
The Travel Insurance Plan provides additional insurance against death, or injury resulting in dismemberment or total and permanent disability, sustained by accident anywhere in the world whilst on company business and during the course of any bona-fide business trip. All permanent employees are covered from day one.
Pension
ExampleCompany operates a non-contributory pension plan.

You are entitled to join the pension scheme after one year of continuous service. You will be contacted by the HR department to arrange a meeting with the Company's Independent Financial Advisor who manages our plan.

Please note pension contributions are based on base salary only and bonuses should not be included when calculating contribution amounts.
Share Scheme.
Through ExampleCompany’s share scheme you can become a company shareholder. This voluntary plan is open to employees who have worked one years service and at least 20 hours a week.

On joining you contribute a proportion of your basic salary to buy shares at a preferential rate.
Alternative Work Policy
If you are temporarily incapacitated due to injury or ill-health so that you are unable to perform your normal duties, the company will always try to make suitable alternative work available.

This can assist your rehabilitation since skilled medical help may be more readily available at your work location than at home, and will maintain your basic earnings.

Under no circumstances would you be allowed to accept such duties if medical opinion suggests that there is a danger of retarding your recovery or aggravating your condition.

Other Benefits
A Sports and Social Committee has been established. For further information please contact Human Resources.
