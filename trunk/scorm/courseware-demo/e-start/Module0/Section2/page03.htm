Courtesy Cars
Some hotels have courtesy cars, which will pick up and return travelers from and to an airport at no charge.
Some may provide transportation within a limited area of the hotel.
Public Transport
Tubes, trains, trams, buses and coaches.
Taxis
Use only where no other less expensive means of transport is available. Employees are encouraged to share a taxi wherever practical.
Personal Cars
Employees may use personal cars for business travel when other transportation is unavailable or uneconomical. The cost will be reimbursed at the local prescribed mileage/kilometre rates.
Insurance
Employees using personal cars on company business are required to maintain the required minimum level of insurance at their own expense.
Parking
Parking of private cars in a long-term car park, while on company business, will be reimbursed if for less than five days. Airport short-term car parks should not be used. Employees are encouraged to use the most economical parking available and should consider alternative means of transportation (taxi/rail) to the airport for extended trips, i.e. over five days
Rail Travel
Standard Class only is permitted on all rail journeys.
