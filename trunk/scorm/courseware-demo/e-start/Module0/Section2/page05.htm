Payment of Incurred Expenses
Where the employee is due expenses, then an expense form needs to be completed.
  • One expense form should be submitted each month. Each Expense Form should be submitted on hard copy signed by the line manager, accompanied by all receipts and statements, where appropriate.
  • A project code must be entered for all expenses incurred.
  • The reference field should be used to cross-reference your receipts with your expense form.
  • Relevant receipts should included in an envelope and attached to the form.
  • Travel should include all travel related costs, excluding food and drink.
  • Subsistence covers food and drink
  • If the expenses incurred do not fit in to any of the categories then use Other.
  • Expense forms must be submitted to the Finance Department by 12.00 on the 20th of each month. Failure to submit your expense claim on time will result in payment being delayed to after the end of the month.
