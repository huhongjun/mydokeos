Jane walks past a taxi but it is unlicensed so she wisely keeps on walking.

N.B. A licensed minicab will have the following displayed:
  1. Two Door Stickers - one at each side
  2. Two Plates - one back and front
  3. Once inside the vehicle the driver will have a badge, which should be worn on his/her person.
  4. A small plate displaying the same number as the plates outside back and front, fixed inside on the dashboard.
  5. Small window sticker with test dates, vehicle number and registration number.
Jane sees a busy pub and decides to walk into it.

Walk into a pub or caf�, or somewhere there are lots of people. If you are being followed, you will be safer amongst groups of people.
