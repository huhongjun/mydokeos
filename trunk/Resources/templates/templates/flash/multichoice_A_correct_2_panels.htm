To change the resolution on an Apple Mac…
1) From the Finder, select menu item: Apple Menu > Control Panels > Monitors.
2) Make sure that the 'Show' pulldown under Resolution says Recommended.
3) Single click on your choice, for example 800 x 600.
4) Close the window.
